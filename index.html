Calculating Power

The Shapley-Shubik power index analyzes and calculates the relative power of any individual voter within a yes/no voting system by observing pivotal* voters. To determine the Shapley-Shubik power of a voter, the number of arrangements with all voters in the system where the voter is pivotal is counted. The total Shapley-Shubik power is calculated by finding the total number of arrangements of all voters in the system. Finally, the Shapley-Shubik index is calculated by dividing a voter's Shapley-Shubik power by the system's total Shapley-Shubik power.
*pivotal refers to a player that has the power to determine the outcome of the election
