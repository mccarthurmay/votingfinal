Comparison of All Three Indexes

Banzhaf Power Index

  • Calculates power via critical voters, or swing voters
  • Observes winning coalitions
  • Most commonly applied to situations where players form coalitions to win

Shapley-Shubik Power Index

  • Calculates power via pivotal voters
  • Observes permutations containing all voters
  • Most commonly applied to situations where players form coalitions to win

Deegan-Packel Power Index

  • Calculates power via individuals in minimal winning coalitions

  • Designed to be more adaptable to encompass a broader range of scenarios
  • Does not require situations where cooperation is the primary driver of decisions
  • Does not require coalitions

  • All three indices measure power in weighted voting games
  • They provide information on how much an individual matters in the case of deciding outcomes
